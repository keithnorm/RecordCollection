Record Collection

Because your music defines you.

Note: As with any third party Spotify app, you must be a Spotify Premium member to use Record Collection. Trust us, it's worth it!

Remember your your stack of vinyls from 1968? Your CD binder from 1995?

Digital media is awesome. We can listen to our favorite songs any time, anywhere. But, we still long for those evenings, sitting around with friends, where you'd flip through their album collection and show off yours. We have our favorites. Those albums which will always be on standby: to enjoy, to remember, or to make you want to rock out. Now we've brought you that same feeling flipping through your record collection to a new iOS app.

Record collection app was created for you (and any music lover):

  • Collect and play all your favorite music
  • Integrates with your Spotify Premium account
  • Sync your collection via iCloud to all your devices
  • Albums in your collection are available offline*

* up to 3,333 tracks per device, per Spotify's restrictions
